Policy

At Payroll, protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy are our top priorities. We comply with the guidelines set forth in the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

Our privacy policy details the use of your personal data. We are committed to privacy and employ measures to safeguard your information.

Personal Data Collection

When you submit inquiries or requests to us, either manually or electronically, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Contact information

Payroll uses data and analytics to examine web traffic, ensuring that this data remains confidential and is not shared with any other parties. The information analyzed includes:

  • Visitor IP address
  • Visitor operating System
  • Search terms used
  • Pages and internal links
  • Date and time of website
  • Geolocation
  • Referring site or platform
  • Web browsing type

Data Usage

The personal data we collect is used solely for documentation and processing within Payroll. This data is crucial for improving our services, maintaining accurate records, and ensuring smooth operation of payroll processes. We do not share your personal data with external parties.

Protection Measures

We prioritize the security of your personal information. Only authorized Payroll personnel have access to this data, and we employ advanced cybersecurity measures to store it securely. Our protection measures include encryption, firewalls, and secure access protocols to prevent unauthorized access and ensure data integrity.

Access and Correction

You have the right to request a copy of your personal information that we have on file. If you find any inaccuracies in your data, you can request corrections. We are committed to ensuring your information is accurate and up-to-date.

Feedback

We value your feedback regarding our privacy notice. If you have any suggestions or concerns, please feel free to share them with us. Your input helps us improve our policies and better protect your privacy.
